# LinkForge Demo Video — Recording Script

## Equipment & Setup
- **Screen recorder:** Loom (loom.com) — free, or OBS Studio
- **Browser:** Chrome (recommended for clean UI)
- **Screen:** Full browser window (hide bookmarks bar for cleaner look)
- **Microphone:** Any decent mic — quiet room, speak clearly
- **Length target:** 4–6 minutes

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:15] OPENING — Show the Landing Page

**ACTION:** Open linkforge.digital in your browser. Let the page load fully.

**SAY:**
> "If you're an SEO professional or website owner, you know that internal linking is one of the most powerful — and most neglected — parts of your strategy. Today I'm going to show you LinkForge, an AI-powered tool that makes internal linking completely effortless."

**ACTION:** Slowly scroll down to show the hero section, features, and "How It Works" section.

**SAY:**
> "LinkForge works with any website — WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Ghost, or any custom-built site. No plugins required. Let me show you how it works from the inside."

---

### [0:15–0:45] SIGN UP & ONBOARDING

**ACTION:** Click "Get Started Free" or "Sign In". If already signed in, sign out first, then sign up with a test email.

**SAY:**
> "Getting started takes just a few seconds. Enter your name, email, and password — that's it. No credit card required."

**ACTION:** After sign-up, the onboarding wizard appears. Walk through Steps 1–3.

**SAY:**
> "Right after you sign up, LinkForge walks you through a quick three-step onboarding. Step one explains the flow — you add your site, LinkForge crawls it, and then you get smart link suggestions. In step two, you enter your website URL. And step three — the crawl begins automatically."

**ACTION:** Wait for or skip through the crawl animation.

**SAY:**
> "The crawl runs in the background. LinkForge discovers your pages, reads the content, and analyzes semantic relationships. For larger sites this might take a minute or two."

---

### [0:45–1:30] DASHBOARD & SITES

**ACTION:** Once on the Dashboard, point to each KPI card.

**SAY:**
> "Here's your dashboard. At a glance you can see your total sites, pages indexed, pending suggestions, and links you've applied. These stats update in real time as you work."

**ACTION:** Click "Sites" in the sidebar.

**SAY:**
> "Let's look at the Sites view. Here's where you manage all your websites. Each site card shows you the platform — in this case WordPress — the crawl status, page count, and when it was last crawled."

**ACTION:** If you have a site, hover over it and show the dropdown menu.

**SAY:**
> "From here you can trigger a new crawl, jump straight to suggestions, or manage the site. If you need to add another site, just click 'Add New Site' in the top right."

---

### [1:30–2:45] SUGGESTIONS — The Core Feature ⭐

**ACTION:** Click "Suggestions" in the sidebar.

**SAY:**
> "Now here's where the magic happens — Suggestions. This is the heart of LinkForge."

**ACTION:** Show the filter tabs (All / Pending / Approved / Rejected / Applied).

**SAY:**
> "You can filter by status. Pending suggestions are ones you haven't reviewed yet. Approved means you've accepted them. And Applied means they're live."

**ACTION:** Point to a suggestion card. Zoom in slightly if needed (use browser zoom Ctrl+).

**SAY:**
> "Each suggestion shows you the source page and the target page — the page that should receive an internal link. The anchor text is highlighted here in orange — this is the clickable text that LinkForge's AI has generated to read naturally within your existing content."

**ACTION:** Point to the relevance score.

**SAY:**
> "Every suggestion comes with a relevance score. Green means high relevance — above eighty percent. You can confidently approve these. Amber is moderate, and red means the connection is weak."

**ACTION:** Click the Approve (✅) button on a pending suggestion.

**SAY:**
> "Approving is one click. You can also reject suggestions that don't fit, or use 'Approve All' to batch-approve everything at once."

**ACTION:** Show the Export dropdown (CSV / JSON).

**SAY:**
> "Once you're happy with your selections, export them as a CSV or JSON file. You or your developer can then implement these links on your site."

---

### [2:45–3:45] PAGES & ORPHAN DETECTION

**ACTION:** Click "Pages" in the sidebar.

**SAY:**
> "Let's switch to the Pages view. This shows you every page LinkForge has discovered on your site."

**ACTION:** Point to the Orphan Pages stat card (highlighted if any orphans exist).

**SAY:**
> "This is critical — the orphan page count. These are pages with zero incoming internal links. Search engines may never discover them, which means all that content is essentially invisible."

**ACTION:** Click the "Orphan Only" filter.

**SAY:**
> "With one click, you can filter to see only orphan pages. These are your priority — every single one of these pages needs at least one internal link pointing to it."

**ACTION:** Point to the word count and headings on a page card.

**SAY:**
> "Each page card shows the title, URL, word count, headings, and link counts. This helps you prioritize which orphans to fix first — start with your highest-quality content."

---

### [3:45–4:30] ANALYTICS & LINK NETWORK GRAPH

**ACTION:** Click "Analytics" in the sidebar.

**SAY:**
> "The Analytics view gives you a bird's-eye view of your internal link structure."

**ACTION:** Show the Link Network Graph. Pan and zoom slightly.

**SAY:**
> "This interactive network graph is one of LinkForge's signature features. Each node is a page on your site. Orange nodes are orphan pages — they have no incoming links. Teal nodes are well-linked pages — your content hubs. You can pan, zoom, and hover over any node to see details."

**ACTION:** Show the KPI cards and Link Health chart.

**SAY:**
> "Below the graph, you see your key metrics — total internal links, pages indexed, orphan count, and average links per page. The Link Health chart shows your progress over the last seven days."

---

### [4:30–5:00] SETTINGS & PRICING

**ACTION:** Click "Settings" then the "Plan" tab.

**SAY:**
> "When you're ready to upgrade, head to Settings and the Plan tab. You can see your current plan, and browse all available tiers."

**ACTION:** Show the plan cards briefly.

**SAY:**
> "Starter is free forever with one site. Pro at forty-nine dollars a month gives you five sites and AI-powered suggestions. Business at one-forty-nine gives you twenty-five sites, analytics, and API access. And Enterprise is custom for larger organizations."

---

### [5:00–5:30] CLOSING

**ACTION:** Navigate back to the Dashboard or the landing page.

**SAY:**
> "And that's LinkForge — from adding your site to getting actionable, AI-powered internal link suggestions in just a few minutes. No plugins, no manual work, just smarter internal linking."

**ACTION:** Point to the "Get Started Free" button.

**SAY:**
> "If you want to try it yourself, head to linkforge.digital and start with a free account — no credit card required. Thanks for watching, and happy linking!"

---

## Tips for a Great Recording
- **Speak slowly** — aim for 120–140 words per minute
- **Pause** after each major section (2–3 seconds)
- **Use your mouse cursor** to point at things as you mention them
- **Don't rush the crawl animation** — let it play, it shows the product is "working"
- **If you make a mistake**, pause for 3 seconds, then re-say the line (easy to edit out)
- **Smile when you speak** — it comes through in your voice!
