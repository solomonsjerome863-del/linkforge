One of the most powerful features in LinkForge is the interactive link network graph. Instead of scrolling through spreadsheets, you can see your entire site's internal link structure visualized as a force-directed graph — and it's fully interactive. The color coding makes it immediately actionable. Orange nodes represent orphan pages — pages with zero incoming internal links. These are the pages Google may never discover, and they're the biggest quick-win opportunities. Teal nodes represent your well-linked pages, your strongest content hubs that are already distributing authority effectively. You can pan around, zoom in on clusters, and hover over any node to see detailed information — including word count, total links, and link counts. Below the graph, you'll find key metrics at a glance: total internal links across your site, how many pages are indexed, how many orphans need attention, and your average links per page. In this example, you can see 182 orphan pages out of 386 indexed — nearly half the site needs internal linking work.
