Welcome to the LinkForge product demo. LinkForge is an AI-powered SEO tool that helps website owners improve their internal linking structure. It crawls your site automatically, discovers orphan pages that have zero internal links, and generates smart, contextual link suggestions with optimized anchor text. This demo will walk you through how the tool works, from adding your site to getting actionable link recommendations. Let's dive in and see how LinkForge can help boost your search rankings by fixing one of the most overlooked SEO problems — orphan pages.
