So how does LinkForge actually work? It comes down to three simple steps. Step one: you add your website URL. That's it — no plugins to install, no API keys to configure. LinkForge is platform agnostic and works with WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Ghost, or any custom-built site. Step two: our engine goes to work. It starts by reading your sitemap.xml to discover every URL on your site, then it actually crawls and reads the content of each page. We use AI to analyze the semantic relationships between all your pages — understanding topics, context, and which pages are topically related. Step three: you get smart link suggestions. The AI generates internal link recommendations with optimal anchor text that reads naturally, plus a relevance score so you can prioritize, and surrounding context so you know exactly where each link fits. It's really that straightforward.
