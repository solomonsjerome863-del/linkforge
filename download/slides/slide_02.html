Here's the core problem LinkForge solves. Studies show that nearly half — 47 percent — of pages on a typical website have zero internal links pointing to them. These are called orphan pages, and they're a massive, often invisible SEO problem. When a page has no internal links, Google's crawler may never discover it, meaning it won't appear in search results at all. Even for pages that are found, without internal links, there's no page authority flowing to them — each page is essentially competing on its own. And fixing this manually? It's incredibly tedious. You have to read through pages, figure out which ones should link to each other, write the anchor text, and then update the content. It doesn't scale beyond a handful of pages. That's exactly why we built LinkForge.
