This slide highlights one of the most critical features of LinkForge — orphan page detection. Studies show that nearly half of all website pages have zero incoming internal links, meaning Google may never discover or rank them. That 47% figure is an industry average backed by multiple SEO audits. LinkForge solves this in three ways. First, Instant Detection: every orphan page is automatically flagged with a warning badge across all views — whether you're browsing the Pages list, reviewing Suggestions, or looking at the Analytics graph. You don't have to go looking for orphans; they find you. Second, Filter & Sort: once detected, you can quickly isolate orphan pages and sort them by word count, URL, or title. This makes it easy to prioritize — for example, you might want to link high-word-count pages first since they likely represent your most valuable content. Third, One-Click Fix: the AI engine suggests the best internal links for each orphan page based on semantic relevance. You simply review, approve, and export. No manual guesswork required.
