Here's our pricing — four tiers designed to grow with you. Starter is completely free forever: one site, fifty pages, and a hundred suggestions per month. It's perfect for trying LinkForge on a personal blog or small project. Our most popular plan is Pro at forty-nine dollars a month. You get five sites, five hundred pages, and two thousand AI-powered suggestions. This tier also includes auto-apply for one-click implementation and priority support. For agencies and growing teams, Business at one forty-nine per month gives you twenty-five sites, five thousand pages, ten thousand suggestions, plus bulk actions, full analytics, API access, and white-label capabilities — ideal if you're serving multiple clients. Finally, Enterprise is custom-priced for large organizations that need SOC 2 compliance, SSO, a plugin SDK for custom integrations, and a dedicated support contact. All plans start with a fourteen-day free trial, and you can cancel anytime. There are no hidden fees, no long-term contracts, and no credit card required to get started.
